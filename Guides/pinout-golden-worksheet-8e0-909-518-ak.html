Use this page as your single source-of-truth worksheet while you move from community pinout hints to factory-confirmed and bench-confirmed assignments.

RULE: A pin is not "true" until it is backed by exact factory current-flow docs and/or bench verification notes.
How To Use
Workflow
  1. Fill the Factory Doc Ref column with exact page/wire references from OEM current-flow docs.
  2. Mark Factory Confirmed only when you have exact page evidence.
  3. Record continuity checks and live behavior in bench columns.
  4. Keep variant-sensitive pins (especially 21 and 121) flagged until proven.
  5. Promote any row to "golden" only after at least one hard proof source.
